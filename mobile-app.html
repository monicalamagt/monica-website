Stock Movement Prediction

Overview

Multimodal neural network framework predicting stock returns by combining technical price features with FinBERT-processed financial sentiment. Compared early vs. late fusion architectures and MSE vs. NDCG ranking losses on 100 stocks spanning 2008–2016 (205K samples).

Findings

  • NDCG loss ties best-in-class performance using 84% fewer parameters than a 6x more complex model
  • Late fusion more consistent across market regimes; early fusion higher risk/reward
  • Outperformed SPY (11.7%) and QQQ (12.9%) over 18-month out-of-sample period
  • Time-series cross-validation revealed insights missed by simple train/test splits
